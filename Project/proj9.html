In celebration of the Feast of St. Michael Xavier Chapel, the entire SK Council of Lower Bicutan joined in the fun of Palarong Pinoy, brought to us by our beloved Mayor Ate Lani Cayetano and the entire City Council.

The youth of Lower Bicutan are truly grateful for the various prizes and games prepared by the City of Taguig together with SK Lower Bicutan, SDO, and BAO.

TARA, KABATAAN! is an inspiring youth engagement initiative by the Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Lower Bicutan. This project brings together 200 young participants from the community, fostering unity, leadership, and community involvement through various interactive workshops, team-building activities, and forums. With a focus on empowering the next generation, TARA, KABATAAN! encourages youth to take an active role in shaping a brighter future for Barangay Lower Bicutan.

We also extend our gratitude to the cooperation of the Mauling Youth Organization - Area II, led by Clodualdo Sevilla Jr., for a smooth and successful event.
