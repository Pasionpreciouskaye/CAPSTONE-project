SIKCUP GAMES 2024

A big congratulations to the teams who triumphed in the SIKCUP GAMES 2024 CHAMPIONSHIP GAME for the following categories:

🏀 3x3 BASKETBALL MEN’S DIVISION 🥈 1st Place: ALLSTAR 🏆 Champion: ONE BLOOD BANAI

🏐 VOLLEYBALL WOMEN’S DIVISION 🥈 1st Place: TAMAGOTCHI 🏆 Champion: QUATRO

🏐 VOLLEYBALL MEN’S DIVISION 🥈 1st Place: SEXBOMB 🏆 Champion: ACES

🏀 5x5 BASKETBALL MEN’S DIVISION 🥈 1st Place: T5 🏆 Champion: TAMPIYUY

A warm greeting from the Office of the Sangguniang Kabataan of Lower Bicutan and from our Barangay Captain Roel Pacayra, as well as our Barangay Councilors who attended the Championship Game and Awarding Ceremony of the SiKCup Games 2024 — Councilors Alvin Cruz, Lester Padilla, and the whole Barangay Lower Bicutan Council — together with the SK Lower Bicutan Council led by: - SK Chairwoman June Lyn Tabanao and SK Kagawads!

To all the athletes of the SIKCUP GAMES 2024, thank you for displaying sportsmanship and cooperation with your fellow players. We also extend our gratitude to all the committees who helped us successfully conduct the SiKCup Games 2024, and to the Barangay Security Force (BSF) for ensuring the safety of everyone.

May the Lord bless you all!
