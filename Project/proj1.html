Project Overview

The "Seed to Lead" initiative empowers young individuals to progress from being team players to visionary leaders. Through workshops, mentorship, and exposure to community leadership roles, this program aims to create the next generation of leaders who are actively involved in their communities and ready to tackle challenges in innovative ways.
