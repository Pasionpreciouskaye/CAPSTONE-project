Project Overview

SEED TO LEAD – FROM TEAM PLAYER TO VISIONARY LEADER

The Sangguniang Kabataan - Lower Bicutan, Taguig, led by SK Chairwoman June Lyn Tabanao and the council, successfully held a meaningful training for active youth who wish to enhance their leadership skills.

SEED TO LEAD – FROM TEAM PLAYER TO VISIONARY LEADER is a seminar and training focused on nurturing the potential and capabilities of the youth when it comes to leadership. The Sangguniang Kabataan of Lower Bicutan extends its heartfelt gratitude to all the young individuals who participated, as well as to our Barangay Captain Roel O. Pacayra and the Barangay Council.

Programs like this for the youth would not be possible without your support.
