For the Youth of Barangay Lower Bicutan who want to represent our Barangay in the upcoming Taguig Sports League 2025, this is your chance to showcase your talent and skills in your chosen sport (BILLIARDS • DARTS • CHESS).

Simply visit the Sangguniang Kabataan Lower Bicutan Office Hall and submit the following requirements:

🎱 Billiards (5 Women Players and 5 Men Players):

Valid ID 2x2 Picture Voter's Certificate

🎯 Darts (10 Women Players and 10 Men Players):

Valid ID 2x2 Picture Voter's Certificate

♟ Chess (10 Women Players and 10 Men Players):

Valid ID 2x2 Picture Voter's Certificate
