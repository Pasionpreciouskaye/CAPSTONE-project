Explore the amazing projects we've been working on!

Community Outreach Program

This project focuses on making a positive impact in the local community by providing support and resources to those in need.

Environmental Awareness Campaign

An initiative aimed at raising awareness about environmental issues and promoting sustainable living practices in our area.

Sports

This project aims to create a sports league that brings together youth from various communities to foster teamwork, competition, and a healthy lifestyle through organized sports events.

SAGOT KO, ISKUL SUPPLIES MO!

TSAGOT KO, ISKUL SUPPLIES MO project to support students in their educational journey. With a mission to empower local vouth and lessen the financial burden on families, this initiative provides essential school supplies to 500 students in the community.

EQUALITY PRIDE

Marching for Colorful Equality & Love is a vibrant celebration of the LGBTQ+ community, uniting 100 youth from Lower Bicutan. Through advocacy, unity, and colorful displays, this march promotes equality, acceptance, and pride, reinforcing that diversity strengthens our community.

SIGLA NG KABATAAN- AMAZING RACE 2024

This exciting event organized by the SK of Lower Bicutan, this event gathers 100 youth for a day of fun, teamwork, and friendly competition. Through relay challenges and interactive tasks, participants build endurance, foster engagement, and strengthen community spirit.

SEEN IT, CLEAN IT

The SEEN IT, CLEAN IT initiative by the Sangguniang Kabataan of Lower Bicutan aims to promote cleanliness and environmental awareness in the community. Through this project, three labeled trash cans--biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclable--are provided to every active basketball court in Lower Bicutan. environment for all residents.

SAGIP KALIKASAN- KABATAAN URBAN GARDENING WORKSHOP

The seminar will teach youth (ages 15-30) practical skills in growing plants, herbs, and vegetables in limited urban spaces. Participar will learn eco-friendly gardening techniques such as container gardening and composting, empowering them to contribute sustainability and environmental care in their communities.
